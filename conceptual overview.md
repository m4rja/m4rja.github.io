# **Conceptual Overview of Akinator**




 

## **Audience:** 

> This overview is written for both contributors and personal users alike. Both audiences will learn how the program, Akinator, works and how the AI functions to be able to guess the characters you want to be guessed. 


## **System Requirements:**

> The program only requires a working device (i.e PC, mobile device), access to a web browser like Google Chrome or FireFox, and/or a mobile device to download the app. 
> The app is available on both iOS and Android systems. 




 

## **What Akinator Does:**

> Akinator is a guessing game that uses questions to guess what you're thinking of. You have the choice of characters, objects, or animals for the genie to guess. You then go through a series of yes or no questions that the program asks. It usually takes an average of twenty questions for Akinator to give a guess. If guessed correctly, you say yes, giving the program a win. If not, it will give you the win and give you the option of answering who the character, object, or animal was.





 

## **How Akinator Works:**

> Akinator is a program that also uses crowdsourcing and databases to create these guesses. The questions the program asks are all created from past users who have input the characteristics of a character, object, or animal. You, the user, have the option of adding the character that they thought of into the database simply by putting in the name after they have defeated the program. You are also able to add pictures and descriptions of the character they wish to add to the database for a better experience for future users who want to guess the same character. This option allows Akinator to have thousands of characters, objects, and animals in their database and continue to amaze new users who may be experiencing the program for the first time.




 

## **Akinator for more Experienced Users/Contributors**: 

 

> If the character you had in mind isn’t guessed correctly, you can add that said character to the database by: 

> 1. Inserting the name of the character, object, or animal.  
> 2. Providing a description or characteristics of your character.  
> 3. Providing questions specific to the traits of the character you have in mind.  
> 4. Inserting an image to visualize it.   
> 5. Agreeing to the terms and service provided by the site. 





 

You ***CANNOT*** add:

1. Yourself and personal information as an option.   
2. A friend or family member and their personal information.

 

Due to safety and security as well as the function of the program, it is advised not to put non-public figures into the database.
